> Tenis Court Reservation Website <

This project was done by myself and 2 of my teammates(Benjamin Kopf and Derim Belica) which is a web-based system for a local tennis club to streamline membership management, court reservations, and financial tracking. The platform allows members to log in, reserve courts under specific restrictions, and manage payments including annual fees, initiation fees, guest charges, and late penalties. The system includes role-based dashboards for members, treasurers, and admins. It was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQLite. The platform is browser-based, requires an internet connection, and emphasizes usability for general users, while offering advanced features for admins and treasurers

> Muscle Force Calculator <

The Muscle Force Calculator is a Python-based application developed by myself and my research team at the University of Hartford to estimate the force exerted by the biceps during arm movements using real-time pose detection. Funded by NASA for the Connecticut Space Grant Consortium, the project utilizes the MediaPipe library to analyze body posture through a camera feed and calculate muscle force based on torque generated by the arm as a lever. Key factors for this to happen include the distance from the elbow to the hand and the bicep's attachment point, along with calculating the distance the person is from the camera while only using 2D visual data. The application features an augmented reality overlay that visualizes muscle forces in motion, making it a valuable tool for biomechanics education, physical therapy, sports science, and fitness training.
